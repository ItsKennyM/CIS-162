Locations to visit




SAN DIEGO ZOO

San diego zoo is a wonderfull place to visit. Claiming to be the "Best zoo in the world!" It has a lot to offer. I recommend going early in the morning to see all the animals. I dont recommend paying for the safari part it is so much walking and only a few things to see, most of the cool things cost extra!














AQUARIUM OF THE PACIFIC

MY personal favourite place to visit. The Aquarium of the pacific is what i believe to be the best aquarium in the world. And although i have only been to 2 aquariums i belive it still true. Growing up this place has given alot of memories. The year long pass is definetly worth it and alot of the money goes to helping the animals! Also being in a perfect spot by the beach there are countless wonderful restaurants and eventful things. you can even go whale watching nearby.
