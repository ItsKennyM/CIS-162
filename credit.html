    How to use Credit cards
    Using credit cards to get you free things

    I use credit cards to book nice places for free. I took me a while and ive made many mistakes but now i travel for free.

    so when looking to get credit cards its either going to be for, geting points or building credit. Never get a credit card if you only need it to buy something you cant afford. That is how you go into debt. Personally i dont have any store credit cards like a target red card or a best buy store credit card. I only use bank cards since those provide the best points.

    ALWAYS look for promotions. In the cards i recommended (ill add more with good promotions later on) there are a few that have promtions. The chase sappire has a promotion of 60k points id recommend to wait if you dont have it already because around holidays they bump that promotion to upwards of 100k points. That can be a whole trip to ANYWHERE. The promotions always make you spend a certain amount of money in the first few months.

    Sometimes waiting to get a card will make it easier. I wait til i know i have alot of expenses to get a card. Before i bought my computer i applied for a card and then use that purchase along with paying for my books to claim the free bonus points

    NEVER claim your points as cash. you lose so much value trying to get cash. If i cashed all my points i would have gotten $200 but using my points and transferrring to a hotel company like Grand Hyatt i was able to get a $2000 stay for free.
